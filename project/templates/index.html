Bangladesh

A country
of natural beauty



The beauty of Bangladesh is a rich tapestry of natural landscapes and cultural heritage, including the world's longest natural sea beach at Cox's Bazar, the massive mangrove forest of the Sundarbans, and vast, verdant river plains. The country also boasts scenic hills in the northeast and southeast, numerous archaeological sites, and lush tea gardens, complemented by a vibrant culture and serene rural environment.
